PClassic 2017 Fall Summary

PClassic took place on November 18th, 2018. We had over 300 students on 70 teams from 30 schools making this the largest fall contest to date!

The top 3 teams this year in the Standard division were:
3) Montgomery Blair High School - Arman Siddique, Noah Singer, Siddharth Taneja, Andrew Komo
2) Unaffiliated - Chris Lambert
1) West Windsor-Plainsboro High School North - Ezra Edelman, Jacob Edelman, Jakob Degan, Eitan Zlatian

Congratulations to the winning teams! Prizes included trophies and free space on Dropbox!

This year's question set is online and the test cases and solutions are available as a zip file.
